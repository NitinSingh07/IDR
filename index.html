Who We Are

An Industry-Led Institute
Built for Real-World Risk

The Institute of Digital Risk is a specialist training and deployment organisation, built by industry for industry. We exist to close the critical gap between traditional education and the rapidly evolving demands of digital, cyber and technology risk roles across regulated and high-consequence environments — from financial services to critical national infrastructure.

What sets IDR apart is the integration of deep academic insight — anchored through formal partnerships with leading UK universities — with practical, project-based learning on live industry challenges. Our practitioners don't just study risk; they experience it, model it, and solve it alongside the organisations that face it every day.
